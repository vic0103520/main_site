Elementary Logic and Automata Theory

Instructor: Bow-Yaw Wang

This lecture covers requisite materials from logic and automata theory. Specific topics include: semantics of classical logic, compactness theorem, completeness theorem, B&uuml;chi automata, satisfiability, validity, and QBF.
